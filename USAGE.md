# How to use Patient Deidentifier

Open the app in your browser (e.g. from the link you were given). No account or install needed.

---

## Steps

1. **Choose your file(s)**  
   Click **“Choose one or more images”** and select the scanned document(s) to deidentify. Supported formats: PNG, JPEG, TIFF. You can select one file or many.

2. **Optional: add names to redact**  
   If you want a specific name redacted everywhere it appears (e.g. “Angie” or “Hall, Angie”), type it in the box and click **Add**. You can add more than one name.

3. **Run deidentification**  
   Click the blue **Deidentify** button. The app will read the text from your image(s) and redact names, addresses, and birth dates. This may take about a minute per image.

4. **Download the result**  
   - **One file:** Click **Download redacted text** to save a single `.txt` file.  
   - **Many files:** Click **Download all as ZIP** to save a ZIP with one redacted `.txt` per image.

---

## What gets redacted

- **Names** (e.g. after “Patient’s Name:”, or names you add under “Custom names to redact”)
- **Addresses** (e.g. street, city, zip)
- **Birth date** (e.g. after “DOB:” or “Date of birth:”)

Other information (e.g. visit dates, clinical notes) is left as-is.

---

## Privacy

Everything runs in your browser. Your images and the extracted text are **not** sent to any server; they stay on your device until you choose to download the redacted text.
